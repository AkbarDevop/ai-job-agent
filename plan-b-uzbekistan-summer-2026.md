A comprehensive research brief for international students and engineers interested in Uzbekistan's tech sector, with focus on AI/ML, engineering, and startup opportunities.

## 1. Tashkent Tech & Startup Ecosystem

Uzbekistan's tech ecosystem is booming. In 2025, the country entered StartupBlink's **Global Top 100** startup ecosystems for the first time, ranking **#1 in Central Asia** and **#2 globally by growth rate**. Tashkent specifically saw a **132% increase**, becoming the fastest-growing tech city in the region. Startup funding jumped **111.92% in 2025** vs. prior year.

### IT Park Uzbekistan (the hub of everything)
- **Website:** [it-park.uz](https://it-park.uz/en/itpark)
- 14 branches nationwide, 2,000+ resident companies, 225,000 sqm of office space
- 1,500+ active startups, collectively raised $300M+
- **Tax exemptions until 2040:** corporate tax, income tax, VAT, customs duties — all exempt for IT Park members
- Free office space for 1 year, salary reimbursement up to 15% ("Zero Risk" program)
- Apply for membership at [my.it-park.uz](https://my.it-park.uz)

### Accelerators & Incubators
| Program | Focus | Details |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Plug and Play Uzbekistan** | FinTech, Enterprise AI | Silicon Valley accelerator, 4 batches completed. Batch 5 likely in 2026. Apply at [pnp.tc](https://innovationchallenge.pnp.tc/int-accelerator-uzbekistan/) |
| **IT Park Ventures** | AI/ML, FinTech, EdTech, SaaS, DeepTech | $10M fund. Investments from $10K (idea) to $1M (Series A). Avg ticket: $150K. Apply at [itparkventures.uz](http://itparkventures.uz/en) |
| **TumarisTech Central Asia** | Women in tech, general | 2 acceleration programs + business analytics school |

| **President Tech Award** | General tech startups | Cash prizes up to $100K per category winner |

### Government Co-Financing
- Government **matches foreign VC investments up to $100K** for startups attracting overseas capital
- Reimbursements for participation in top-tier global accelerator programs
- Non-collateral loans up to ~$23,500 (300M UZS)

## 4. Remote Work for US Companies

### Feasibility: High
Uzbekistan has a growing remote work infrastructure. Key platforms listing Uzbekistan remote jobs:
- [DailyRemote](https://dailyremote.com/remote-software-development-jobs-in-uzbekistan) — 32+ new remote dev jobs as of March 2026
- [Arc.dev](https://arc.dev/en-uz/remote-jobs) — matches skills/time zones with global startups
- [Himalayas](https://himalayas.app/talent/countries/uzbekistan) — remote talent marketplace
- [Jobgether](https://jobgether.com/remote-jobs/uzbekistan) — 25K+ remote jobs accessible from UZ

### Rate Expectations
- Remote dev rates from Uzbekistan: **$20-60/hr** (general), **$40-100/hr** (AI/ML specialist)
- Upwork, Toptal, and Turing are active platforms
- AI-related freelance work grew 60% YoY; AI freelancers earn 44% more than generalists

### Key Considerations
- **Time zone:** Tashkent is UTC+5 (11-14 hours ahead of US Pacific/Eastern). Overlap with US East Coast: ~6-9 AM Tashkent = evening US ET. This makes async work or early-morning sync calls viable
- **Tax:** Self-employed in IT Park-registered entity = tax exempt. Otherwise, check freelancer self-employment regulations (67 exempt professions since 2020)
- **Internet:** Improving rapidly under Digital Uzbekistan 2030 but verify speeds at the specific location
- **Payment:** Wise, Payoneer, PayPal (limited), crypto via Telegram Wallet (launched in UZ)

## Top 5 Actionable Moves (Ranked by Impact)

### 1. Apply to Plug and Play Uzbekistan Batch 5
Build an AI product (even a rough MVP) before summer. Apply to the next Plug and Play cohort. This offers Silicon Valley-tier mentorship, investor access, and IT Park backing — all in Tashkent. Strong AI/ML skills and a working prototype make for a competitive application.

### 2. Cold-Email New Uzbekistan University AI Lab
Their AI cluster is brand new, backed by the president, partnered with MIT/TUM. International students and engineers with CV/ML experience wanting to do summer research are exactly their target. Contact the [Institute of Fundamental and Applied Research](https://www.ifar.uz/new/lab_ai.html).

### 3. Register at IT Park + Build in Public
Register a legal entity as an IT Park member (tax-free until 2040). Build an AI product targeting the Uzbek market (Telegram bots, CV for retail/agriculture, EdTech). Apply to IT Park Ventures for seed funding ($10K-$150K).

### 4. Attend Global Tech Weekend (May 15-17)
Network with founders, investors, and tech professionals. Speaker applications may still be open. Engineers with hands-on AI experience can often secure workshop or speaking slots.

### 5. Teach AI Workshops at IT Park
The government is spending $100M on AI education and needs trainers. International university backgrounds combined with hands-on AI experience and English fluency are rare in Uzbekistan. Propose a workshop series on practical AI/ML (Python, CNNs, computer vision) to IT Park's education team.
